# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This is a community project for grassroots cricket clubs, and we want taking part to be a
friendly and welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background,
or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that helps:

- Being patient and welcoming, especially with people new to coding or streaming.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the clubs using the project.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a club setting.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests, and discussions — and when
representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by contacting the maintainers through
the club website linked on the repository. Maintainers will review and respond, and may
remove comments, commits, or contributions that violate this code, or block contributors for
serious or repeated breaches.
